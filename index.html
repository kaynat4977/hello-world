SCRAMBLED EGGS

egg are one of my favourite food. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tabs butter
  • 2 tabs cream

METHODS

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Grently mix the eggs and cream in a bowel
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
